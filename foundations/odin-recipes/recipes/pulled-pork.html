Pulled Pork

Desciption

This is a simple recipe to make delicous pulled pork. The prep time for this is minimal and the results are mouth watering.

Ingredients

  • Pork Butt
  • Mustard
  • Seasoning
  • Apple Juice

Steps

  1. Slather the pork butt in mustard
  2. Use your seasoning of choice, and spread over the pork butt
  3. Heat smoker to 225 degrees and place pork butt fat side up on to grate
  4. Heat pork butt to 200 degrees (8-12 hours)
  5. Remove from smoker and wrap in tin foil until warm
  6. Shred meat adding apple juice and more seasoning to taste. (Apple juice makes the meat more moist if dry)
  7. Enjoy!
